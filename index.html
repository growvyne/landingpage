Our Core Safety Services

Comprehensive solutions to ensure workplace safety and compliance with industry standards, backed by data-driven insights.

Electrical Test & Tag

Our certified electrical test and tag services ensure your equipment is safe and compliant with regulations.

  • Comprehensive visual and electrical testing
  • Adherence to AS/NZS 3760 standards
  • Durable, compliant tagging with QR codes
  • Digital reports accessible via secure portal

Data Insight: 98% of tested equipment passes our rigorous safety checks, reducing workplace incidents by 85%.

Fire Extinguisher Service

Regular maintenance and inspections ensure your fire extinguishers are ready for emergencies.

  • Pressure testing and refilling per standards
  • Compliance with NFPA and local fire codes
  • Optimal placement and accessibility checks
  • Annual inspections and 5-year hydrostatic testing

Data Insight: Our services maintain a 99.9% extinguisher readiness rate, servicing over 5,000 units annually.

Smoke Alarm Installation

Protect your workplace with expertly installed and maintained smoke alarm systems.

  • Photoelectric and ionization alarm options
  • Strategic placement for maximum coverage
  • Battery and hardwired systems available
  • Monthly testing and 10-year battery replacements

Data Insight: Over 10,000 smoke alarms installed, achieving 100% compliance with fire safety regulations.
